Steve Jobs

The Visionary Behind Apple

Steve Jobs was a pioneer of the personal computer revolution. He co-founded Apple Inc. and changed the world with his innovations in technology, design, and business.

Timeline of Steve Jobs' Life

  • 1955: Born in San Francisco, California.
  • 1976: Co-founded Apple with Steve Wozniak and Ronald Wayne.
  • 1984: Launched the first Macintosh computer.
  • 1985: Left Apple and founded NeXT.
  • 1997: Returned to Apple and launched the iMac.
  • 2001: Introduced the iPod.
  • 2007: Unveiled the first iPhone.
  • 2011: Passed away, leaving behind a legacy of innovation.
